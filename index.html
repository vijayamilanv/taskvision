Role-Based Workflow Management

Each role has specific responsibilities and permissions

Product Owner

Strategic role that defines projects, sets priorities, and approves plans. Creates projects and assigns them to Project Managers.

  • Create Projects
  • Set Priorities
  • Approve Plans
  • Monitor Progress

Project Manager

Execution role that breaks down projects, assigns tasks, and monitors progress. Manages deadlines and coordinates with team members.

  • Break Down Projects
  • Assign Tasks
  • Monitor Deadlines
  • Report Progress

Team Member

Operational role that executes tasks, updates status, and completes work. Collaborates with team and reports progress.

  • Receive Tasks
  • Update Status
  • Collaborate
  • Complete Work
